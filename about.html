Our story

Building the Lowcountry Since 1989

Paul Langan founded the company on a simple idea: homeowners deserve a contractor who treats their home like his own. More than 35 years later, that principle still guides every renovation, remodel, and repair we take on across Hilton Head Island and Bluffton.

We're a true full-service contractor. Whether you need a single room refreshed or a whole-home transformation, Paul personally oversees the work as your general contractor or project manager — coordinating permits, painting, carpentry, roofing, concrete, plumbing, electrical, and flooring so you never have to juggle a dozen vendors.
